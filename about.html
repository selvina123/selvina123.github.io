About Me

I’m passionate about turning data into secure, actionable insights that drive smarter decisions and stronger defenses. At Syracuse University, my focus lies at the intersection of data analytics, cybersecurity, and cloud technologies where numbers meet networks. At Orion Secure, I work on real-world cybersecurity initiatives from risk governance and vulnerability assessments to incident response planning. I’ve gained hands-on experience with tools like Kali Linux, CentOS, Metasploitable2, Nmap, and OpenSSL, building skills in ethical hacking, penetration testing, and cryptography. On the analytics side, I love exploring data stories using Python, R, SQL, Tableau, Power BI, and Excel. My projects range from energy forecasting and AI-powered systems to interactive Shiny dashboards, combining technical precision with creativity. Certified in Google Data Analytics and Prompt Design in Vertex AI, I’m also expanding into AWS, GCP, Azure, and Snowflake to strengthen my cloud foundation. I’m always curious, always learning and motivated to build solutions where data intelligence meets digital defense.
